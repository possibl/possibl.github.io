what is veX?

VEX is both hands-on and minds-on STEM learning. The VEX Robotics Program encourages innovation, team work, self-confidence, communication and leadership. We build, code, program, document and compete with student designed robots. This year's Vex Competition is called Tipping Point, with the object of the game to strategize as part of a team alliance to score more points than the opposing alliance - this includes scoring rings, moving mobile goals to alliance zones, and by balancing on tipping platforms at the end of the match. We will participate in virtual and live competitions. Learn more at vexrobotics.com

Our goal

Our goal is for every interested student to be able to participate in Robotics. With your help, we are able to offset the costs of team registrations ($150/ team), competition fees ($500/ team), transportation to and from competitions ($450/ bus), team t-shirts ($300), and robot kits ($3,000-5,000).

Your sponsorship makes a difference

With your sponsorship support, we are giving a new generation of STEM learners a hands-on opportunity for learning and competing. Sponsors over $250 will be recognized on our team banners - proudly displayed in the classroom and in competition - and through FHS social media channels.
